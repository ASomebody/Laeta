Our Mission

Stress is all around us, in the form of an approaching deadline or an event that did not go the way we planned. Although everyone faces some form of stress, we deal with our stress differently. Laeta is designed to help users find happiness in their own ways. Our website offers hassle-free activities such as reading positive messages, listening to relaxing music, or scrolling through soothing images.


Who We Are

We are four dedicated, aspiring computer scientists who want to develop new technology to solve problems around the world. We are currently participating in the Girl Who Code Summer Immersion Program at Sephora.
